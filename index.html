Pops sayings

Every family has idioms, that no one in the family knows how or where they started. here are some of the sayings I grew up with, most of these sayings were uttered by my father, who was a man of few words.

  • Dont eat S$%+, bigger than your head.
  • A ha ha nest fulla teehee eggs
  • You just don't believe Grits is grocery
  • Dont take no wooden nickels
  • Bunches and bunches, a bushell and a peck
  • You are the receipt

Don't eat sh%+, bigger than your head

I do believe, this may have been my fathers favorite saying, and no it isnt dietary advice. Pop would use this term whenever he felt there was a chance we were taking on more than we could handle. Most parents would say "dont bite off more than you can chew".

A ha ha nest fulla teehee eggs.

Growing up whenever we were having fun, especially if we were rough housing, Pops would say this. The translation is you're laughing now but your going to be crying later. It was understood by all the kids not to bother Pops with whatever event caused said tears. Unless it was funny...

You just dont believe Grits is grocery

This one was usually said as a warning, sometimes sternly, sometimes with a chuckle either way it meant FAAFO.

Dont take no wooden nickels

Simply put, dont be anyones fool.

Bunches and bunches, a bushell and a peck

Started out as "I love you bunches and bunches, a bushell and a peck." eventually it was shortened to a call and response, where pops would say "bunches and bunches" and 'we would say a bushell and a peck",to close it out he would say and a "hug around the neck"

You are the receipt.

This is by far my favorite of my fathers saying. The weight this saying carries stays with me to this day, it is a saying that I say to my kids constantly. this was my fathers way of making sure we knew that no matter how big the problem, we could handle it. the full saying is your ancestors already paid the price, you are the receipt. The meaning is that all of those who came before you stand behind you, and support you. Therefore there is NOTHING you cant accomplish.
